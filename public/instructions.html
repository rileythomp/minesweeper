Minesweeper

  • The goal is to safely reveal every non-mine square without clicking on a mine as quick as possible.
  • If you click on a square with a mine, you lose.
  • If you click on a non-mine square, it will reveal the number of mines in the 8 squares that surround it.
  • If there are no mines surroudning that square, it will reveal all the squares surrounding it recursively.
  • Using the number of mines surrounding a cell, you can deduce which cells are safe to click on.
  • You can flag a square you know has a mine on it by right clicking, and unflag the cell by right clicking again.
  • You can click hint to show a square that does not have a mine on it.
  • You can click solve to partially solve the board.
