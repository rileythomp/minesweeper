Minesweeper

  • Your goal is to clear all of the safe reveal every square without clicking on a mine.
  • When you click on a square, it will reveal the number of mines surrounding it.
  • If there are no mines surroudning that square, it will reveal all the squares around it recursively.
  • Using the number of mines surrounding a cell, you can deduce which cells are safe to click on.
  • You can mark a cell with a mine on it by right clicking, and unflag the cell by right clicking again.
  • You can click hint which will show a square that does not have a mine in it.
  • Solve will help partially solve the game from its current state.
